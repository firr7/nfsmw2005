Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2005) is an open-world street racing game developed by EA Black Box. You start at the bottom of the Blacklist — a roster of 15 of Rockport's most notorious street racers — with a stolen BMW M3 GTR to reclaim. Your mission: beat every rival, evade the police, and reclaim your place at the top.

// Cultural Impact
The Legacy
Still the Gold Standard for Street Racing Games

Many racing fans consider Most Wanted (2005) the high point of the classic Need for Speed formula. Its fusion of cinematic police pursuits, deep customization, and a compelling progression system through the Blacklist continues to inspire fan projects, modding communities, and modern racing game designs.

The game's aesthetic — gritty urban environments, a pumping soundtrack, and iconic rival characters like Razor — left a cultural mark that endures two decades later.
